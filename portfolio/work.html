Snake

The snake game is a classic arcade game where the player controls a growing snake on a grid using the the arrow keys, aiming to eat food and avoid collisions with the walls or its own body. The goal is to survive as long as possible and achieve the highest score.

Vocable

Vocable is a word-guessing game where players attempt to deduce a randomly selected five-letter word. By making guesses and receiving feedback on correct and incorrect letters, players aim to uncover the target word within a limited number of attempts.

Blackjack

Blackjack is a card game where players try to get a hand value on or close to 21 without going over. Players are dealt cards and decide to "hit" for more or "stand" to beat the dealer's hand. If your cards add up to a number higher than the dealers you win the game.
